2. Description of Services

PatientTracForge is a cloud-based EMR scheduling and revenue cycle management platform that includes:

  • Patient registration, demographics, and insurance management
  • Multi-provider appointment scheduling with AI-powered optimization
  • Encounter creation, clinical routing, and cross-app integration (Revela, Mental Health)
  • Billing module including superbills, EDI 837P submission, ERA 835 posting, and A/R aging
  • Five AI models powered by Anthropic's Claude: no-show prediction, smart scheduling, intake assistant, wait time tracking, and billing AI
  • Email (Resend) and SMS (Twilio) communications infrastructure
  • Admin portal with role-based access control and TOTP MFA
  • Specialty clinical modules: Revela (plastic surgery) and Mental Health (behavioral health)
