Not AI bolted on after the fact. Five Claude models that fire automatically — at booking, check-in, intake, encounter close, and billing.

No-Show Prediction
Highest ROI · Fires at booking
Every appointment scored 1–10 for no-show risk the moment it's booked. Claude analyzes prior no-show history, insurance type, appointment day and time, visit type, and time since last visit. High-risk patients (7+) get staff alerts and automatic extra reminders. Critical (9–10) prompts double-booking recommendation.
5–15%
of revenue lost to no-shows — recovered

⏱
Wait Time Intelligence
Patient Experience · Real-time
Four timestamps tracked per visit: arrived → called back → seen → departed. Wait time calculated automatically. Dashboard surfaces averages by provider, day of week, and hour. Patients who wait more than 20 minutes receive an automatic apology SMS via Twilio. Providers see weekly performance summaries every Monday morning.
20 min
threshold — auto apology SMS fired
03
📅
Smart Scheduling
Efficiency · At booking
Before booking, Claude analyzes the provider's 14-day schedule, the patient's no-show risk, insurance visit interval requirements, and specialty patterns. Returns three ranked optimal slots with reasoning. Automatically flags double-bookings, insurance denials from too-frequent visits, and high-risk time slots (Monday AM for high-risk patients).
3
ranked slots suggested with conflict detection
04
📋
AI Intake Assistant
Time Savings · 48h before visit
Patients receive a tokenized 48-hour link before their visit. A Claude-powered chat collects chief complaint, duration, severity (1–10), current medications, and allergies. AI returns a clinical summary pre-filling the encounter, suggested ICD-10 codes, and triage level — emergent, urgent, or routine — with specific red flag alerts.
5–10 min
saved per visit on front-desk intake
05
💊
Billing AI
Revenue Cycle · On encounter close
When an encounter closes, Claude reads the chief complaint, provider specialty, appointment type, and patient insurance — then suggests CPT codes with confidence scores, ICD codes, modifiers, and flags denial risk 1–10 with specific reasons. Biller reviews and accepts with one click. "This visit type with this insurance has a 34% denial rate — consider adding modifier 25."

denial rate detected and flagged before submission
